Welcome to Pazar.bg

Pazar.bg is an online marketplace for all kinds of goods. Here you can browse thousands of offers, uploaded by people all across Bulgaria. You can send private messages to sellers to ask for offer details before buying, and the seller can reply. After you buy an offer, you can see it in the "Bought offers" page. You can also upload your own offers for others to see and buy.
